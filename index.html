Final Project: Autonomous manure grinder for a turkey farm

The problem: The floor where the turkeys are raised, known as a bed, is made up of rice husks, organic matter and humidity. It is an ideal environment for flies to lay their eggs. 95% of the flies are in a larval and pupal state, and only 5% are adults. This 5% is a problem for raising birds. The solution: A robot that locates areas with high humidity, detects groups of larvae and kills them mechanically. Mechanics: Servo, motors and gears. Electronics: Motion, humidity and temperature sensors. Programming: Data processing and decision making
